🚗 MINI Inspired by Goodwood — Production Register

A community VIN registry for one of the most exclusive MINI special editions ever built.


About This Project
The MINI Inspired by Goodwood was a limited edition of just 1,000 vehicles, produced between November 2011 and July 2012 in collaboration with the Rolls-Royce Motor Cars craftsmen at the Home of Motor Racing — Goodwood. Available in just two colours, Diamond Black (909 units) and Reef Blue (91 units), and distributed to 23 markets worldwide, each car was a hand-finished piece of automotive art.
This register has been years in the making. What started as a personal passion project grew into a meticulous cross-referencing of build records, owner submissions, enthusiast forums, and community contributions from across the global MINI Goodwood community. Every entry has been individually reviewed and verified — a genuine labour of love for a truly special car.

The Web Registry
The centrepiece of this project is a fully self-contained, single-file HTML registry page — no server, no database, no dependencies. Just open it in a browser.
Features:

All 1,000 VINs searchable and filterable in real time
Filter by colour, country, transmission, or search by VIN directly
Sortable columns — click any header to sort ascending or descending
Paginated at 50 records per page for clean browsing
Production breakdown charts — destination markets, colour split, transmission and steering
Animated diamond sparkle hero, styled in the spirit of the car itself
